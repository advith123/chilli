Five-step Pipeline

Field to FOB.

See the full 15-step lifecycle →

  1. 01 — SOURCE

    Direct from farm

    Procured from contract farmers and trusted yards in Warangal, Khammam, Enkoor and Guntur. No middlemen, traceable lots.

  2. 02 — DRY

    Sun-cured

    Spread on yards for 5–7 days. Moisture brought below 10%, the threshold for export-grade longevity.

  3. 03 — SORT

    Hand-graded

    Sorted by length, colour, stem-condition and broken percentage. Foreign matter removed under tube-light inspection.

  4. 04 — PACK

    Export-ready

    Jute bags (25/50 kg), PP woven, vacuum cartons or private-label retail packs. FCL or LCL.
