02 — Soil to Port

A journey of 147 days.

  1. 01

    Contract Farming

    We partner with 1,200+ farmers across AP, Karnataka, and Kashmir on fixed-price seasonal contracts. No middlemen, full traceability.

  2. 02

    Hand Harvest

    Fruit picked at peak color — typically 110 days after transplant. Ripe pods only; immature fruit is rejected at the field itself.

  3. 03

    Sun Drying

    14–21 days on raised bamboo platforms. Moisture drops from 78% to a stable 10%. No artificial dehydration, no color enhancers.

  4. 04

    Grading & Stemming

    Manual and machine-sorted into Grade A/B/C by size, color, and defect count. Stems removed for EU and US-bound shipments.

  5. 05

    Lab Testing

    Every lot tested for aflatoxin, Sudan dye, moisture, ASTA color, and SHU. NABL-accredited lab reports accompany every container.

  6. 06

    Port & Beyond

    FCL and LCL shipments out of Chennai, Mundra, and Nhava Sheva. FOB, CIF, and DDP incoterms available on request.
